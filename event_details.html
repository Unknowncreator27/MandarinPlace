About this event

This event is hosted in Victoria Park on July 25th every year to celebrate us and give back to the community who help us become the best noodle restaurant in town.

During this event, participants will have 15 minutes to eat as many bowls of noodles as possible (each bowl holds 250g of noodles). Who ever eats the most bowls will be crowned the winner!
